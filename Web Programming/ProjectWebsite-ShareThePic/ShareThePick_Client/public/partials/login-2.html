About

ShareThePic allows users to create events, invite other users to those events where everyone has the ownership of the event and can upload and share pictures to that event. In addition they can comment on the uploaded images. ShareThePic is focused on providing a platform for event based image sharing where multiple users assume the ownership of the uploaded event specific images.
